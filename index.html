VendorSync Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

VendorSync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, company name, and role (planner, vendor, or guest)
  • Profile information: Instagram handle and website URL (optional)
  • Project content: wedding timelines, documents, chat messages, and media you upload
  • Device information: Expo push notification token for sending notifications to your device

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the VendorSync platform
  • To send push notifications about timeline updates, new documents, and chat messages
  • To facilitate communication between planners, vendors, and guests within a project

Data Sharing

  • Your information is only shared with other members of projects you are invited to
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Files and documents are stored in Supabase Storage.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

VendorSync is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us.
